About Us

Smooth Talkers is a Chilean language school, founded in february 2012.

We are experts in customized leanguage education and work with teachers from different backgrounds and nationalities, including non-Spanish speakers who also learn Spanish with us. We have different options for you: private, group and online classes. Our teachers are certified, experienced and mindful of students' needs.

We offer classes in 6 different levels, from beginner to advanced, and all our teachers are native speakers. A course consists of a minimum of 8 classes and all materials are provided.
